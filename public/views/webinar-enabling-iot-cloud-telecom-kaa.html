Accelerating Successful IoT Applications and Services in Telecom With Kaa

Posted Jul 11

Telecom operators and communications service providers are driving some of the largest Internet of Things deployments in the world. Whether they are working with systems integrators or directly with enterprises, whether they are mobile or fixed Internet service providers, their solutions teams are often taking the lead in designing, engineering, implementing, and managing sensor-based machine-to-machine networks.

During this webinar we looked into new business opportunities opened for Telecom operators by the Internet of Things. The keystone of our presentation was the IoT cloud – the essential foundation of every IoT initiative. We explained how it can be properly implemented and what use cases it can enable for Telecom companies. Also we illustrated 5 specific IoT business cases for Telecom operators in more detail and went through the IoT implementation stories from some well-known Telecom operators.
